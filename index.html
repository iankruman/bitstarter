The Game of Life is a new way to create, track, update and share your personal goals. Create milestones and "level up" when you reach them. Share your accomplishments with friends on TGOL or your favorite social platform. TGOL let's you define your goals with any degree of depth or difficulty. Want track your progress to a personal best fitness goal? Find 30 new music artists which interest you? Days straight without breaking your brownie fast? Track it all with TGOL.

Not feeling particularly creative? Browse a set of already created goals/milestones for inspiration. Better yet, see what goals are trending in TGOL network. If you do create goal set, publish it so that other users can join you in your quest to achieve your dreams. Keep track of your progress with built in graphic tools to see your trends. Enjoy your success as you see your results manifest before your eyes.
